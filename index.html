About

Hi, my name is Avery, and I am the owner and founder of Private STEM Coaching. Over the course of my 4+ years working as a private tutor, I successfully taught a range of students with many different backgrounds and goals. I founded PSTEM Coaching because I recognize that each student is unique, and we here at PSTEM Coaching pride ourselves on catering to all of our students' individual needs and preferences.

When hiring coaches from PSTEM, you can expect high quality lessons with qualified subject experts. Most of our coaches either have or are in the pursuit of their Bachelor's Degrees, and many also have plenty of relevant industry experience. You can view their profiles in the 'Profiles' section and select the one the suits you best.
